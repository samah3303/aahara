# Free LLM Alternatives to OpenAI

Yes, you can absolutely use free alternatives! Here are the top 3 recommendations for this project.

## 1. Google Gemini API (Recommended) üåü

- **Cost**: **Free** (up to 15 requests/minute).
- **Quality**: Very smart (Gemini 1.5 Flash is fast and accurate).
- **Setup**: Easy. Get a key from AI Studio.
- **Pros**: Generous free tier, very stable.
- **Cons**: Data can be used for training (in free tier).

## 2. Groq (Llama 3) ‚ö°

- **Cost**: **Free** (currently in beta).
- **Quality**: Extremely fast. Uses Llama 3 (Meta's open model).
- **Setup**: Get a key from Groq Console.
- **Pros**: Blazing fast speed (instant replies).
- **Cons**: Free limits might change in the future.

## 3. Ollama (Local) üè†

- **Cost**: **100% Free Forever**.
- **Quality**: Depends on your computer (Run Llama 3 or Mistral locally).
- **Setup**: Install software on your PC.
- **Pros**: Private, no internet verification needed.
- **Cons**: Uses your computer's RAM/CPU. Computer must be ON.

## My Recommendation

**Switch to Google Gemini.**
It is the most reliable "Cloud" option that is free to start.
